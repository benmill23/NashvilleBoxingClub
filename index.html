Our Story

Nashville's Premier Private Boxing Experience

Founded in 2026, Nashville Boxing Club represents the pinnacle of exclusive fitness in Music City. We've created a sanctuary where elite athletes, entertainment industry professionals, and discerning individuals train side by side with world-class coaches.

Our facility combines the raw authenticity of a championship boxing gym with the discretion and amenities expected by our distinguished clientele. Privacy, performance, and personalized attention define every aspect of the Nashville Boxing Club experience.
